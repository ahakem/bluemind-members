Privacy Policy

YOUR PRIVACY MATTERS

Blue Mind Freediving (BMF) is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data.

Effective Date: December 2024

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register as a member:

  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, phone number, residential address
  • Date of Birth: To verify age requirements and manage age-appropriate activities
  • Emergency Contact: Name, relationship, and phone number
  • Identification: National ID or passport number for verification purposes

1.2 Medical Information

For safety purposes, we collect limited medical information:

  • Medical certificate confirming fitness for freediving
  • Medical conditions that may affect diving safety
  • Insurance provider information
  • Blood type (optional but recommended)

1.3 Freediving Information

  • Certification level and certification numbers
  • Training history and session attendance
  • Personal best records and performance data
  • Equipment information

1.4 Financial Information

  • Payment history and invoices
  • Membership fees and payment status
  • Bank transfer details (when provided for payment verification)

1.5 Digital Information

  • Login credentials (encrypted)
  • IP addresses and device information
  • Usage data and activity logs
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

2.1 Membership Management

  • Processing and managing your membership
  • Verifying your identity and eligibility
  • Maintaining accurate membership records
  • Managing renewals and membership status

2.2 Safety and Training

  • Ensuring participant safety during training sessions
  • Emergency contact and medical information access
  • Tracking certifications and skill levels
  • Assigning appropriate training groups

2.3 Communication

  • Sending session schedules and updates
  • Important safety announcements
  • Administrative notifications
  • Responding to your inquiries and requests
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)

2.4 Financial Operations

  • Processing membership fees and payments
  • Generating invoices and payment records
  • Managing refunds and credits

2.5 Legal Compliance

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Responding to legal requests and court orders
  • Protecting our legal rights and interests
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized activities

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Information

BMF does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.2 When We Share Information

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Emergency Situations: Emergency contacts, medical personnel, or authorities in case of injury or medical emergency
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (e.g., payment processors, cloud storage, email services) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Venue Operators: Pool or training facility operators for access and safety purposes
  • Insurance Providers: In case of insurance claims (with your consent)
  • Certification Bodies: For verification of certifications (with your consent)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Access Control: Role-based access restrictions ensure only authorized personnel can access sensitive data
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure cloud servers (Firebase/Google Cloud Platform)
  • Regular Backups: Automated backups to prevent data loss
  • Security Audits: Regular security reviews and updates
  • Password Protection: Strong password requirements and secure authentication
SECURITY NOTICE

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and notifying us immediately of any unauthorized access.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Active Membership: All data is retained while your membership is active
  • After Membership Ends: We retain essential records (name, email, membership history, signed waivers) for 7 years for legal and liability purposes
  • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years as required by law
  • Medical Certificates: Retained for 3 years after membership ends
  • Marketing Data: Deleted upon request or after 2 years of inactivity

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal information we hold about you.

6.2 Right to Correction

You can update or correct your personal information through the member portal or by contacting us.

6.3 Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records.

6.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

6.5 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for marketing communications or photo/video usage at any time.

6.6 Right to Object

You can object to certain processing of your personal information.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@bluemindfreediving.com

  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how members use the portal

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect functionality.

  • Refuse further collection of their child's information

10. International Data Transfers
